About Our Idea

Our Idea is a career counselling website that helps people find their ideal career. We do this by taking inputs from users based on their interests and taking tests.

The tests are aptitude and personality tests, which help us determine the interests of the person. Our model then predicts the best career for the person as per the scores in tests and personal preferences.

We offer a personalized experience. We take into account each person's unique interests, skills, and personality traits to make sure that we recommend the right career for them.
